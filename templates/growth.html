Growth of Marijuana Industry in Colorado, Washington and California

Califonia: 1996 Medicinal Legalization

California voted on Proposition 19 in November 2010, which would have legalized Marijuana similar to Washington and Colorado, but the bill did not pass. Although the bill failed, they are likely to legalize in 2016 and they paved the way for other states by introducing potential legislation.

Colorado: 2014 Recreational Legalization

Colorado passed Amendment 64 in November 2012. The amendment covered both personal use of marijuana as well as retail sales. The first retail stores opened on New Year's Day in 2014. Marijuana tax sales were almost as high as $1B for 2015. Let's include a graph of month by month sales to show the growth of the industry.

Washington: 2014 Recreational Legalization

Washington passed Washington Initiative 502 in November 2012. Sales began for the most part in July 2014.
