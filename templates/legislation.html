Ballot Process

While the ballot iniative process varies state to state, 24 (source) states have some sort of process for citizens to submit new laws or constitutional amendments to undergo a popular vote during the next election. In most cases, an initial proposal is submitted, after certain number and type of petition signatures must be collected in order for the initiative to go to a ballot vote.
