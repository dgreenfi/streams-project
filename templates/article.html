Colorado: 2014 Recreational Legalization

Colorado passed Amendment 64 in November 2012. The amendment covered both personal use of marijuana as well as retail sales. The first retail stores opened on New Year's Day in 2014. In most cases, an initial proposal is submitted, after certain number and type of petition signatures must be collected in order for the initiative to go to a ballot vote.
